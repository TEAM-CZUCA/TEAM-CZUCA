## 🛡️ Who We Are

**TEAM-CZUCA (Cyber Zulfiqar Under Cover Agency)** is an independent, non-profit digital ethics organization based in **Bangladesh**.  
We operate voluntarily to combat **online obscenity, atheistic propaganda, and digital moral decay** — promoting a safer, cleaner internet for future generations.

| 🌍 Mission | 🔐 Core Principles | 🎯 Strategic Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Eradicate digital obscenity & anti-faith content | Integrity · Anonymity · Justice · Accountability | A faith-respecting, obscenity-free cyberspace |

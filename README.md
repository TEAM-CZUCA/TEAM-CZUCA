## 🛡️ About TEAM-CZUCA

> **Cyber Zulfiqar Under Cover Agency** is an independent, volunteer-driven digital ethics organization from **Bangladesh**.  
> We combat online obscenity, atheistic propaganda, and digital moral decay through ethical awareness and research.

| 🌍 Mission | 🔐 Core Values | 🎯 Vision |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Eradicate digital obscenity & anti-faith content | Integrity · Anonymity · Justice · Accountability | Faith-respecting, obscenity-free cyberspace |
